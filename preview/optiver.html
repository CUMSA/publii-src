Mitsui Bussan Commodities Ltd

Mitsui Bussan Commodities Ltd (“MBCL”) is an energy derivative market maker based in London with offices in Singapore and New York. We are 100% owned by Mitsui & Co. of Japan. We have over 25 years of experience in provide hedging solutions for our clients, who range from energy producers, refiners, utilities and consumers. Our product offerings are financial derivatives in Oil, Refined Products, Gas, Power, Coal and FFAs in addition to Emissions Allowances.
